Which river is considered the holiest river in Hinduism?

The Ganges River, often called Ganga, holds deep spiritual importance in Hinduism. It's believed bathing in its waters cleanses sins. Many make pilgrimages to its banks for purification rituals.

What is the highest civilian award in India?

The highest civilian award in India is the Bharat Ratna. It is given for exceptional service or performance in any field.
